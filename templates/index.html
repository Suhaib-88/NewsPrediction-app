App Features

  • First App takes in user submitted headlines and predicts the category

  • A headline may belong to one of the following category categories [BUSINESS, POLITICS, WELLNESS, PARENTING, WORLDNEWS,TRAVEL,STYLE & BEAUTY,SPORTS,TRAVEL,FOOD & DRINK,ENTERTAINMENT]. It may also belong to multiple categories at once!!

  • On top of that, App also predicts the tags for a headline. for example: A headline may contain (name of a person, date, org.name etc)
